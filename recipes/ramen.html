Ramen

Hearty and comforting, ramen noodles are a delicious Japanese dish consisting of wheat noodles served in a savory broth, often garnished with various toppings.

The preferred meal of Boruto's Dad, from Boruto

Ramen is best served hot, with the noodles cooked to a firm yet tender texture, immersed in a flavorful broth, and topped with various ingredients

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Veggies like carrots or kale
  • Broth (chicken or veg)
  • Garlic and ginger
  • All your favorite toppings like some panko, egg, chili oil, etc.

Recipe instructions

  1. Soft-boil two eggs and set aside.
  2. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions and drain./li>
  3. In a pot, simmer chicken or vegetable broth.
  4. Add cooked ramen noodles to the broth.
  5. Divide noodles and broth into serving bowls.
  6. Top with sliced cooked pork or chicken, vegetables, and green onions.
  7. Place halved soft-boiled eggs on top.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
